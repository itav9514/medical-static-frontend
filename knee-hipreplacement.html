Types of Joint Replacement Surgeries Offered

We coordinate safe and effective treatments for:

  • Total Knee Replacement (TKR) - Cemented/Uncemented, Minimally Invasive
  • Partial Knee Replacement (PKR) - For localized joint damage
  • Total Hip Replacement (THR) - Anterior, Posterior, or Lateral Approach
  • Bilateral Joint Replacement - Simultaneous surgery for both knees or hips
  • Revision Joint Surgery - For previously failed or worn-out implants
