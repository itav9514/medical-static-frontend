Flight Booking Assistance for Medical Travel

At Adam Vacations, we understand that traveling to another country for medical treatment can be a stressful process — not just for the patient but also for their family or companion. That’s why we offer end-to-end flight booking services tailored exclusively for international medical travelers coming to India.

What We Offer

Affordable Flight Options

We help you book cost-effective flight tickets for both patients and their companions, ensuring safe and comfortable travel to India’s major medical hubs such as Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Indore, and Gurgaon.

Flexible Booking & Rescheduling

We know medical appointments may shift. Our flight partners offer flexible date changes and zero/low cancellation fees for medical travelers (terms apply).

Group & Family Bookings

Travelling with family? We offer special rates for group bookings, ensuring that your loved ones can travel with you at an affordable cost.

Special Assistance During Travel

Need wheelchair access, assistance for elderly patients, or priority boarding? Our team ensures airlines are informed and all arrangements are made in advance.

Airport Transfers & Meet-and-Greet

Upon arrival in India, we also arrange airport pick-up, meet-and-greet services, and ambulance transfers if needed — giving you a smooth transition from air to care.

Supported Destinations

We provide flight booking assistance for patients coming from the USA, UK, UAE, Canada, Australia, South Africa, and many other countries, heading to top Indian hospitals in:

Cities Covered

  • Delhi NCR (Gurgaon, Noida)
  • Mumbai
  • Bangalore
  • Hyderabad
  • Chennai
  • Kochi
  • Ahmedabad

Required Details for Booking

To help us book your flights, please share:

  • Passenger name(s) as per passport
  • Travel dates (tentative or confirmed)
  • Preferred airports (departure & arrival)
  • Medical condition (for assistance needs)
  • Companion details (if any)
