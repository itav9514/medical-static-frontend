What is Stem Cell Therapy?

Stem cell therapy involves using autologous (from the patient) or allogenic (from donors) stem cells to repair or regenerate damaged tissues, modulate immune response, or restore normal function in chronic or degenerative conditions.

India allows approved research-backed treatments, focusing on safety, efficacy, and personalized protocols.
