About Us

Who We Are

Adam Vacations Medical is a trusted, patient-first medical tourism facilitator based in India, committed to connecting international patients with world-class healthcare services across top cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Indore, and Gurgaon. We are a division of Adam Vacations Pvt. Ltd., a global travel and wellness solutions company, and we specialize in offering holistic support for patients traveling to India for medical treatment.

Our mission is to simplify the journey to better health—by offering not just world-class treatment options, but a seamless experience including visa support, flight bookings, hotel accommodations, and even post-treatment care.

Our Vision

To become a leading global partner in medical tourism by making high-quality, affordable, and ethical healthcare accessible to international patients, especially from the USA, UK, Europe, Africa, the Middle East, and Asia-Pacific regions.

Our Mission

To ensure safe, timely, and effective treatment options in India for global patients.

To collaborate only with NABH- and JCI-accredited hospitals and globally renowned specialists

To provide personalized care coordinators for end-to-end support throughout the medical journey.

To ensure transparency in costs, procedures, hospital choices, and travel planning.

Why Choose Adam Vacations Medical?

Top-Tier Hospitals

We work exclusively with India’s most reputed multi-specialty hospitals such as Fortis, Apollo, Medanta, Max Healthcare, and more—ensuring the highest standards of treatment.

Highly Qualified Doctors

Our partner hospitals house world-renowned surgeons and specialists in cardiology, oncology, orthopedics, fertility, cosmetic surgery, neurology, and more.

Global Patient Services

From the USA to Africa to Europe, we support international patients with multilingual staff, video consultations, medical record evaluations, second opinions, and complete logistical support.

End-to-End Travel Support

From visa letters to airport pickups, hotel stays, and translator services, we manage it all so that patients and their families can focus entirely on recovery.

Affordable Healthcare

India offers 60–80% cost savings compared to countries like the USA, without compromising on quality. We ensure transparent pricing and no hidden fees.
