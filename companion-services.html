Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Are your callers based in the United States?
Yes — every single call is made by a live person located in the United States. We do not use offshore call centers or automated systems. You get a real, caring human voice every time.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
Absolutely not. There are no contracts, no commitments, and no cancellation fees. You may cancel your service at any time for any reason, with no questions asked.
What if my loved one doesn't answer the call?
If we are unable to reach your loved one, we will try again and, if needed, contact the emergency contacts you have provided. You choose exactly what you want us to do in this situation — we follow your instructions carefully.
Can I customize the call schedule and reminders?
Yes — all plans are fully customizable. You can specify the time of each call, the reminders to include (medications, meals, appointments, activities), and any personal preferences. Just call us at 1-855-888-0114 to set it up.
How does the Monitored Check-In work exactly?
You simply text, email, or call our toll-free number once per day to let us know you're okay. We review every check-in with a real person. Each evening at 6PM EST and PST, we do a final review. If we haven't heard from you, we'll reach out with a reminder. If there's still no response, a live person calls — and if needed, we alert your designated contacts per your instructions.
Is my personal information kept private?
Absolutely. We take privacy very seriously. All information provided to us is kept strictly confidential and is never shared with or sold to third parties. Your trust is everything to us.
What if standard plans don't fit my needs?
No problem at all! We offer fully custom plans. Whether you only need calls two days a week, want a specific time window, or need a unique combination of services — just call us at 1-855-888-0114 and we'll build a plan around your exact requirements and budget.
Do you provide service across all US states?
Yes — Adam Vacations provides companion care services to clients in all 50 US states. Whether your loved one is in Florida, California, Texas, or anywhere in between, we're here for them.
Are there any setup fees?
No. There are zero setup fees, zero activation fees, and no hidden charges of any kind. The monthly plan price is all you pay. Everything is transparent from day one.
Can I sign up on behalf of an elderly parent or relative?
Absolutely — in fact, most of our clients are signed up by their adult children or family members. You manage the account preferences, receive updates, and are listed as an emergency contact. Your loved one simply receives the calls and check-ins you arrange for them.
