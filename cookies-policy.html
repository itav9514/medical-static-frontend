Last Updated: July 14, 2025

This Cookie Policy outlines how Adam Vacations Pvt Ltd (“we”, “our”, or “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, https://medical.adamvacations.com/index (“Website”). By continuing to use our Website, you consent to the use of cookies as described below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and a personalized browsing experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cookies are essential for you to browse the Website and use its features. They are necessary for the operation of core functions, such as navigating pages and accessing secure areas.

Performance Cookies
These cookies collect information about how visitors use our Website, such as which pages are most visited. The data collected is anonymous and helps us improve how the Website works.

Functional Cookies
Functional cookies allow the Website to remember your preferences (such as your language or location) and provide enhanced, personalized features.

Targeting and Advertising Cookies
These cookies are set by us or our advertising partners. They are used to deliver advertisements relevant to you and to track the effectiveness of our marketing efforts.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use cookies from trusted third-party providers such as:

  • Google Analytics – to collect traffic data and understand visitor behavior.
  • Facebook/Meta Pixel – for retargeting ads and conversion tracking.
  • Hotjar or Clarity – to monitor user interactions anonymously for UX improvements.

These third-party cookies are governed by their own privacy and cookie policies.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies using your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

See what cookies you have and delete them individually

  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies entirely
  • Delete all cookies when you close the browser

However, please be aware that disabling cookies may limit the functionality of the Website and affect your browsing experience.

5. Cookie Duration

Cookies may be stored either for:

  • Session – deleted automatically when you close your browser
  • Persistent – remain on your device until manually deleted or expired
